Runners DJ

A novel music experience for runners and cyclists

Runners DJ is mobile application that will improve your running experience, enhance your motivation and increase your performance. Runners DJ plays the right song that you selected when you need it the most. Approaching the steep and feared ascent to the highest point of your running course Runners DJ will motivate you by playing the sound track with the right beet and energy to get you going all the way up to the top. On the lovely path through the woods by the lake Runners DJ will select a piece of music that will make you feel relaxed and recuperate.

At the track you start the Runners DJ and let it record your path and running pace. At crucial points of the track you can record your heart rate by putting you finger in front of the camera or via a Bluetooth heart rate monitor.

Happily at home you can compose your geographically controlled playlist and select where Runners DJ should change tune. The stride speed and heart rate can be used to let Runners DJ advice you of suitable songs to play on a given part of your track. It is easy to create multiple playlists, each with different cadence. This enables you to change the cadence of your running with a single button push or by biometric data from a heart rate monitor.
